# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This is a welcoming and inclusive learning community. We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, education, or socioeconomic status.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and kind to all contributors and community members.
- Use inclusive language.
- Be open to feedback and constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Show empathy toward other community members.
- Assume good intentions and ask clarifying questions before taking offense.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, bullying, or threatening language directed at individuals or groups.
- Discriminatory jokes or comments based on protected characteristics.
- Unwelcome sexual advances or attention.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Trolling, spam, or disruptive comments.
- Any form of violence or threats.

## Reporting & Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report it**: Open an Issue or contact the repository maintainer directly.
2. **Be specific**: Include details about the behavior and context.
3. **Confidentiality**: Your report will be handled with care and in confidence.

Violations may result in:
- A warning and request to remove the offensive content.
- Temporary or permanent removal from the repository.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces associated with this repository, including Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions, and any related communication channels.
